How It All Began

Our Story

The Rescheduled Call

Ask anyone — the man does not text back.

So naturally, scheduling their first call took a few attempts. No expectations. Just two people finally agreeing to pick up the phone.

3 Hours Later...

That "quick call" went three hours.

Same goals, same energy, same wavelength. Usually Sandeep's opener is a 45-minute TED Talk on AI and fantasy football. Chamanthi was interesting enough he never had to pull that card. She still doesn't know how lucky she got.

Every. Single. Day.

They never stopped talking.

For the guy who ghosted group chats for fun, this was historic. Their families noticed. Their friends noticed. The phone bill noticed.

Dallas Changed Everything

They skipped the "getting to know you" phase entirely.

Dates, gym sessions, late night drives, spontaneous adventures — they moved like they'd been together for years. Their families united. It just made sense.

It Starts Here

From a rescheduled phone call to "I do."

The man who couldn't text back found someone worth calling every day for the rest of his life. The best chapter starts now.
