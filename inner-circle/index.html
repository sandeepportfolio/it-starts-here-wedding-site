Our Story

Our Story

01

The Opening

1 of 5

She noticed the guy known for his AI agent setup, slightly controversial takes, and randomly asking if his friends were down to hoop at 9 PM.

So she did what any reasonable person would do… reached out.

Sandeep would probably like it on record that this is where his Hall of Fame texting run began. The truth? It took a second… and then, kind of, it did.

02

THE DELAY

2 of 5

His first thought: “wait… she hella cute.”

His next move: delay the date by a week because he broke his finger… while… of course, playing basketball 🏀.

In Sandeep’s own review of the situation, it was not exactly an all-time opening move.

A week later, what was supposed to be a quick call somehow stretched into almost four hours. The kind of call where you finish and just sit there like… yeah, that was different.

03

THE CLICK

3 of 5

Gym people. School nerds. Both loud in a room. Both chasing something bigger. Same wavelength, just different starting paths.

And after that call, the rhythm changed completely, because not a single day passed without them talking.

04

THE FIRSTS

4 of 5

They met in person on Christmas Day. Yes, actually December 25th. No pressure.

First date: Windmills in The Colony, TX.

Early flirting was elite in its own way:

adjective_to_describe_chamu = “beautiful”
print(“wasssuppp”)

Sandeep called it romance, while Chamu called it Python code.

Then came February 14, 2026. First flowers.

05

BEST FRIENDS ENERGY

5 of 5

To everyone else, they just look like best friends who’ve known each other forever.

Which is funny, because that’s kind of what it is.

Best friends first. Real love underneath. Easy laughs, zero effort, the kind of comfort that usually takes years but somehow showed up early.

The wild part?

“We’re just as surprised as you are!”

— Chamanthi and Sandeep
